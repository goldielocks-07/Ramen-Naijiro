Born from a deep passion for authentic flavors and the spirit of Japanese street culture, Ramen Naijiro was founded by Angelo Derek E. Limpo in 2018 with a simple dream—to bring the rich, comforting taste of real Japanese ramen closer to the everyday Filipino.

What started as a humble street-side ramen stall quickly grew into a beloved culinary gem, thanks to Angelo’s dedication to quality, craftsmanship, and heart. With every steaming bowl of ramen served, Ramen Naijiro offered more than just food—it offered an experience that felt both foreign and familiar, exciting yet soulfully comforting.

Driven by the love and loyalty of its growing community, Ramen-Naijiro has since evolved into one of the most sought-after ramen spots in the Philippines. Known for its deeply flavored broths, perfectly cooked noodles, and signature creations inspired by the vibrant streets of Japan, it continues to capture the hearts—and taste buds—of ramen lovers across the country.

Today, Ramen-Naijiro stays true to its roots while embracing innovation and expansion. With each bowl served, we carry on our mission: to make every bite a story of passion, culture, and warmth. We are committed to bringing the authentic street ramen experience to more communities, one bowl at a time.
